Omelette with Vegetables

An Omelette with Vegetables is a delightful breakfast dish made by whisking eggs, then cooking them with a medley of colorful vegetables. This versatile and nutritious breakfast option offers a perfect balance of fluffy eggs and the vibrant flavors of saut√©ed veggies. With a touch of seasoning, it's a wholesome and customizable breakfast that's quick and easy to prepare.

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 tablespoons of milk
  • Salt and pepper, to tastel
  • 1/4 cup of grated cheese (e.g., cheddar, mozzarella)
  • 1/4 cup of diced vegetables (e.g., bell peppers, onions, tomatoes)
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or cooking oil

Steps

  1. Crack the eggs into a bowl, add the milk, and season with a pinch of salt and a dash of pepper. Whisk the mixture until well combined.
  2. Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat and add the butter or cooking oil. Allow it to melt and coat the bottom of the pan.
  3. Pour the egg mixture into the skillet and let it cook undisturbed for a few moments until the edges begin to set.
  4. Sprinkle the grated cheese and diced vegetables evenly over one half of the omelette.
  5. Carefully fold the other half of the omelette over the fillings, creating a half-moon shape.
  6. Continue cooking for a minute or two until the cheese melts and the omelette is cooked to your desired level of doneness.
